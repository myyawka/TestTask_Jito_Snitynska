Brand Voice

How KostnГ¦r speaks.

Five qualities, held together. The voice is a friend who has read the science and decided not to lecture about it.

i.

Calm

Speak in full breaths. No urgent verbs, no exclamation marks. Pauses are content.

"A small lunch today is enough."

ii.

Intentional

Every word earns its place. We name the action and the reason; we never decorate.

"Add water вЂ” you're a glass behind yesterday."

iii.

Warm

The voice of a kitchen, not a clinic. Soft nouns: bowl, morning, handful, season.

"Slow oats, a spoon of honey."

iv.

Non-judgmental

No "should". No red. Missed days are reported as facts, not failures.

"Yesterday was lighter than your usual. Today, as you like."

v.

Encouraging

Praise the rhythm, not the result. Streaks are described in seasons, not numbers.

"A quiet week of cooking at home вЂ” well done you."
