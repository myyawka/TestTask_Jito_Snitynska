The brief

Two user stories
guided every screen.

01

“As a user, I want to calculate the amount of calories in a dish or a specific product.”

Answered by Search, Food Details and AI Scan.

02

“As a user, I want to find a recipe for a dish that is suitable for me.”

Answered by Discovery, Recipe Details and Saved Recipes.

First touch

A welcome, and a glance.

Two screens to set the tone. The app opens not with a demand for data, but with a sentence. The home screen, once you arrive, holds a single calm idea: where are you today, and what's next.

Flow · User Story 01

Calculate calories for any dish.

Three honest paths to the same answer. Search the database when you know what you ate. Open Details to choose your portion. Or point the camera at the plate and let on-device AI guess for you.

Flow · User Story 02

Find a recipe that suits you.

Discovery as a gentle act. Four filters that match how people actually talk about food. A recipe page that reads like a small printed card. And a saved collection, kept like a private cookbook.

End of chapter

Two stories told in eight quiet screens.

That's Kostnær — a calorie calculator that doesn't shout. Thank you for walking through the case study.
